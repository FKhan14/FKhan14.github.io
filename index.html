About Me

I'm a sophomore at Northeastern University pursuing a dual degree in Data Science and Finance, expected to graduate in May 2028. I'm passionate about building systems that sit at the intersection of machine learning, software engineering, and real-world decision-making. Whether it's predicting tech salaries, matching developers to opportunities, or improving healthcare access for underserved communities, I'm drawn to problems where data can make a meaningful difference.

I have hands-on industry experience from two software engineering internships at Aegis Software, where I contributed to full-stack enterprise systems serving over 100 users across global infrastructure. I worked with REST APIs, MySQL, Kubernetes, Docker, and Vue.js — gaining real exposure to what production-grade software looks like beyond the classroom.

Academically, I'm a National Merit Finalist, University Honors Program member, and consistent Dean's List student with a 3.75 GPA. My coursework spans computer science, data science, databases, and finance, giving me a broad foundation to tackle interdisciplinary problems. Outside of academics and work, I enjoy exploring new datasets, contributing to open-source projects, and thinking about how machine learning can be applied responsibly in high-stakes domains like medicine and hiring.
